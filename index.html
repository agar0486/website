10/2-10/6 Week 7
- This week we focused on bioengineering. We designed and created a prostheic hand. Using string, cardboard and tape we took the idea of tendons within a hand to stretch and pull the fingers. We learned how details and mechanisms are used to preform simple functions such as picking up a water bottle. Then, we created a prostheic for an animal of our choice. I used an elephant and made a replacement leg with a working ankle.
